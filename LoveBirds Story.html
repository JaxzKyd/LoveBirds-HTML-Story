Love Birds



Tylor

was just a little chick when he married Kate







Kate was a little chick too.

One day Kate was worried about Tylor

Tylor wasn't able to fly







It took him 10 seconds to fly to a corner 5 feet away

He was to sick to gather food for the chicks,

the kids started to worry

Nia said, "daddy are you okay?"




he was as sick as a dog.

If he wasn't strong enough

he would fall to the ground like Pow, Pow







Than she said whats 3 times 4 he said 7

that's when she knew something was wrong

Daddy said, "I am okay."

Then Kate came and said, "It's time for you to lay down."

Kate and Tylor were alone and started to talk.







Kate said, "honey are you feeling well?"

"Kate you and I used to be the perfect love birds.", Tylor said.

"We still are", Kate said.

Then Nia came and said, "you still are as long as we are a family"

Then Tylor gave them a huge hug and felt better than ever!







The End

Family is with you through sickness and health.
